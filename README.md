# 英単語の語源図鑑

## administer / mini = 小さい
The teacher administered corporal punishment.  
We are sick and tired of the Mr.T administration.  
- minister / 大臣、牧師  
The Prime Minister is to visit America next week.

- minute / 細かい、綿密な、分、瞬間  
There is a minute difference between the two.

- diminish / 減少する、小さくなる  
His influence has diminished with time.

- minor / 小さい方の、重要でない、未成年者  
Hu suffered minor injuries in the accident.

## advocate / voc = 声、呼ぶ
They advocated reducing the military budget.
He was a strong advocate for educational improvements.
- vocation / 職業、転職  
At last he found a vocation as a writer.
  
- avocation / 趣味、余暇  
His avocation is playing the guitar.
  
- equivocal / 曖昧な、はっきりしない、いかがわしい  
She gave an equivocal answer.
  
- vow / 誓う  
He vowed that he'd never smoke.
